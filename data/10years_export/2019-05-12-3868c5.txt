Gulf of Oman incident, Iran–United States relations, Iran–Saudi Arabia relations: The United Arab Emirates reports four commercial vessels were subject to "sabotage operations" just outside its territorial waters in the Gulf of Oman and two of the vessels suffer "significant damage" to their structure off the coast of Fujairah. One of the vessels was en route to the United Arab Emirates to be loaded with Saudi Arabia crude oil and sent to the United States. (Reuters) (Associated Press) (CNN)
