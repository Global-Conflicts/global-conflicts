Insurgency in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa: Three people are killed and 13 others wounded during an explosion near the residence of Hafiz Muhammad Saeed, the founder of the Islamist terrorist group Lashkar-e-Taiba, in Lahore, Pakistan.
