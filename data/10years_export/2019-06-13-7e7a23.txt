June 2019 Gulf of Oman incident, Iran–United States relations, Iran–Saudi Arabia relations: The United States Central Command (CENTCOM) releases a video showing what CENTCOM spokesperson says, is an Iranian Revolutionary Guard Corps' patrol boat approaching the Kokuka Courageous "and was observed and recorded removing an unexploded limpet mine from the M/T Kokuka Courageous." Iran denies involvement.  (Reuters)
