2015 Bamako hotel attack: Ten gunmen storm the five-star Radisson Blu Hotel in the Malian capital Bamako, taking 170 hostages with at least 27 people killed. The grenade-toting assailants free captives who are able to recite the Quran. The hotel is "often used" by Air France staff. (France 24) (NBC News) (The New York Daily News) (Reuters via Yahoo! News)
